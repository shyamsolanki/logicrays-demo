Winter Season

Winter season is the coldest season which starts from the month of December till the month of March. The peak winter months are January and December.

Winter season is the coldest season of all. The essence of the winter is enjoyed by most of the people Sports such as hockey, cricket are enjoyed playing in the sun. ... The days are shorter & nights are longer. Hot tea, coups, coffee etc are enjoying during the chilled weather.

Winter season comes with fog. Fog covers sunlight and makes winter colder. There is a huge change in atmospheric temperature and routines of day and night in winter. In winters night becomes long and the day becomes short
